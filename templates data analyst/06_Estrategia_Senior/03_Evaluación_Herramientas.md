## Tool Evaluation Framework

### Evaluation Criteria

#### Functional Requirements (40%)
- **Core Features:** Does it meet basic needs?
- **Advanced Capabilities:** Future-proof functionality
- **Integration:** APIs and connectors available
- **Customization:** Ability to adapt to specific needs

#### Technical Requirements (25%)
- **Performance:** Speed and scalability
- **Reliability:** Uptime and stability
- **Security:** Data protection and compliance
- **Infrastructure:** Cloud/on-premise compatibility

#### Business Requirements (20%)
- **Cost:** Total cost of ownership
- **ROI:** Expected return on investment
- **Vendor Stability:** Company financial health
- **Support:** Quality of customer service

#### User Experience (15%)
- **Ease of Use:** Learning curve
- **Interface:** Intuitive design
- **Training:** Required training time
- **Adoption:** Likelihood of user acceptance

### Recommendation Matrix
- **Small Business (<100 users):** Power BI
- **Large Enterprise (>1000 users):** Tableau or Looker  
- **Technical Team:** Looker or Qlik
- **Business Users:** Power BI or Tableau

### Implementation Considerations
- **Migration Complexity:** Effort to move from current tools
- **Training Requirements:** Hours needed for proficiency
- **Change Management:** User adoption strategies
- **Rollout Plan:** Phased vs big bang implementation
